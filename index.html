RANCHI ' THE CITY OF WATERFALLS'

Ranchi, the capital city of Jharkhand offers startling richness and diversity of experience. It is located at an altitude of 2,140 feet in the valley of Chotanagpur. Ranchi has an undeniable charm and its famous waterfalls, verdant hills and forest are excellent spots for photography and other recreational activities. This innovative city is impressively diverse. It is also the leaping off point for the Betla National Park that lures wildlife enthusiasts to this eastern state of India. As compelling as the capital can be, come, spend a few days at Ranchi. Getting around is incredibly easy via road.
